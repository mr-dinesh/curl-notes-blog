## The pitch

- What is the idea in one sentence — if you can't say it in one sentence, you don't have a pitch yet
- Why now — what makes this timely or urgent
- Why you — what gives you standing to write this

## What's in it for them

- What will readers learn that they didn't know before
- Does it fit the publication's beat and tone
- Is there a story here, or just an argument — editors want stories

## Questions to answer before pitching

- What are the gaps in your own understanding — research these first
- Who are the people you need to talk to
- What would make this piece fall apart — address it before the editor does
